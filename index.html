How it works
From brief to production
Find the right factory anywhere in the world, send your brief, and start producing.
01
Search and filter
Search by product type, country, minimum order, and certifications. Every factory on MEx. is verified before going live.
02
Send your brief
Fill in the enquiry form on any factory profile. MEx. forwards your message to the factory by email, and they contact you directly.
03
Need more support?
Hire a MEx. production manager to handle the entire process for you — from sourcing to final delivery.
Every factory is personally verified
We manually check the documentation, certifications, and accreditations of every factory before they appear on MEx. You only see manufacturers we stand behind.
